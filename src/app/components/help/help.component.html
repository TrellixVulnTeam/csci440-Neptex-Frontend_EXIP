Return help

Our mission is to deliver the most compelling shopping experience for our customers.

Please check your packages on arrival. Any concerns regarding your shipment must be report within 10 days of the ship date of your package. Most items purchased from Market America through shop.com may be returned for a full refund, less shipping charges, within 30 calendar days of the date they were shipped to you. Food, custom ordered items and gift cards cannot be returned for a refund for any reason.

If you want to return an item to Neptex E-mart, please contact our refunds department for a Return Authorization Number (RA#) prior to returning your item. You can contact us by e-mail at refunds@neptex.com or call us at (336)-389-2200. Any items you try to return to us without a valid RA# will be shipped back to you at your cost and expense.
